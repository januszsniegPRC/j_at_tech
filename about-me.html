About Me

Hi! I'm Janek, and I spend over 5 months building this site from scratch - using online tutorials, help of my friends or experts like Jakob Nielsen or Steve Krug and my own knowledge I've managed to come up with an idea which was to serve me as a build-up to my CV and university where I aimed to study History Degree.

By this time you're visting this site *hopefully!*, I'll be preparing myself onto diverse path and that is Computer Science. When creating this site I was not certain if people would ever visit it but I'm sure any child out there who is just taking their first steps into the IT world will find this site useful.

I know some people may consider me crazy sharing this site openly on GitHub but I also want you to show what I've done and you can play around with it, trying to understand what each different tag means.

As of myself, I'll be uploading this site but after finishing it I'll be moving onto different projects that I have in mind including; a game and some video related stuff. Perhaps, I'll publish a book that I always wanted to write? We will never know, if you have made this far thank you so much. Also check out my LinkedIn profile ;).

And, to all students out there, you're doing well!
